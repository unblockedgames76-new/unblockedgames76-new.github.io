Super Mario Halloween Wheelie is an exciting online game that adds a twist to the classic Super Mario universe. In this game, our beloved hero, Super Mario, has ventured into the world of motorcycles. With his newly acquired motorcycle license, Mario embarks on a thrilling adventure that requires your assistance to conquer each course. Players are tasked with helping Mario perform a series of tricky stunts with his bike, navigating through an enchanting Halloween-themed environment, and striving to clear all levels. The game's objective is to execute a wheelie stunt all the way to the finish line, but beware, as making the front wheel touch the ground will bring your adventure to an abrupt end.

Super Mario Halloween Wheelie is a user-friendly online game that offers straightforward gameplay. To get started, all you need to do is click on the screen. Once the game begins, your character will automatically move forward on their motorcycle. However, the challenge lies in maintaining a continuous wheelie. To achieve this, you must click on the screen constantly to ensure that Mario's front wheel stays off the ground. This simple mechanic becomes increasingly challenging as you progress through the game's various levels.
